About the Show

The AI Investment Arena

Clank Tank is an AI-powered game show where blockchain entrepreneurs pitch their projects to a panel of simulated crypto judges. Inspired by Shark Tank, our system transforms real business pitches into complete episodes with AI-generated characters and dialogue.

The judges evaluate each pitch, ask tough questions, and ultimately decide whether to "pump" or "dump" the project. It's entertainment with a purpose — giving projects exposure and AI-driven feedback.

Behind the Curtain

How Episodes Are Made

From pitch submission to published episode — our 8-step AI pipeline

Pitch Submission

Entrepreneurs submit project details through our form with tokenomics, team info, and vision.

AI Research

Our system analyzes each pitch using AI for enhanced context and market research.

Character Creation

AI generates a character profile to represent the pitcher in the show.

Script Generation

AI Writers' Room creates complete episode scripts with natural dialogue.

Voice Synthesis

Each character's lines are converted to speech using unique AI voice profiles.

3D Rendering

Episodes are rendered in real-time using PlayCanvas with JSON-based scene control.

Recording

Automated tools capture high-quality video with professional transitions.

Publishing

Automated upload to YouTube with professional metadata and thumbnails.
