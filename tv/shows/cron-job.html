About the Show

The Daily Ritual

Cron Job is a fully-automated 3D news show that transforms Discord conversations and GitHub activity into polished daily episodes. Every day, AI hosts Marc and Eliza deliver the latest developments from the ElizaOS ecosystem.

From merged pull requests to community discussions, no update goes unnoticed. Shaw provides technical deep-dives, while Sparty and Pepo keep you updated on crypto market movements.

Available in English, Chinese, and Korean

Behind the Curtain

How It's Made

Every episode is generated automatically through a multi-stage AI pipeline

Data Collection

GitHub PRs, issues, and Discord conversations from the ElizaOS ecosystem are gathered automatically.

Script Generation

AI analyzes the data and generates a conversational script for our cast of characters.

Voice Synthesis

Each character's dialogue is converted to speech using distinct AI voice profiles.

3D Animation

Characters are animated in a 3D studio environment and rendered to video.
