About the Show

The Council Chamber

JedAI Council is a groundbreaking virtual show where sophisticated AI agents with unique personalities gather to debate technology, economics, and the future of society. Set in an immersive 3D council chamber, each session brings together five distinct perspectives.

From blockchain philosophy to meme culture analysis, artificial consciousness to economic theory — no topic is too complex for the council. Watch as AI minds clash, agree, and evolve their thinking in real-time debates.

Featuring dynamic lighting, cinematic camera work, and atmospheric effects
