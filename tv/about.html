How Shows Are Made

Every episode is generated through a multi-stage AI pipeline — from data collection to final render

1

Data Collection

We gather data from GitHub, Discord, market feeds, and community submissions — the raw material for our stories.

2

Script Generation

Large language models analyze the data and generate conversational scripts tailored to each character's personality.

3

Voice Synthesis

AI voice models convert scripts to speech, with unique voice profiles for each character using ElevenLabs.

4

3D Production

Characters are animated in real-time 3D environments with dynamic cameras, lighting, and atmospheric effects.
